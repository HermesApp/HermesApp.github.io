Hermes

Pandora Client for OS X

Download
For OS X 10.5+
 Develop
It's open source.
 Issues
Report a bug.
Fast
Hermes doesn't use flash. It doesn't get dragged down with all that excessive overhead
and extra bloat.
Free
Even for users who don't have Pandora One, Hermes is a free application which works for anyone who uses Pandora.
Usable
Hermes pauses music when your computer sleeps, and it doesn't resume on wake. It fixes errors and picks up the song right where you left off. Little things make all the difference between Hermes and flash applications.
Features
Nothing is lost from Pandora's website. Everything you could do before is still possible. You can like/dislike songs, create/edit/delete stations.
